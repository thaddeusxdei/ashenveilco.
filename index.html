Yes. I do not change your password, email, or any account details. Everything is done manually — no bots, no scripts, no third-party tools. I recommend changing your password after service is complete for extra peace of mind.
How do I pay? +
GCash for Philippine clients, PayPal for international clients. A 50% downpayment is required for services totaling ₱100 and above. Full payment may be required for smaller services.
How long does it take? +
Depends on the service and scope. I'll give you a time estimate before we start. Rush services are not accepted — I prioritize doing things right over doing them fast.
Will you keep me updated during the service? +
Yes. I send progress updates throughout so you always know what's being done on your account. You can check in anytime via Discord or Facebook.
Can I negotiate the price? +
Sometimes, yes. If you're availing multiple services at once, discounts are possible. Let me know your budget and we can figure something out.
Do you accept international clients? +
Yes! Rates are listed in both PHP and USD. Payment via PayPal for international clients. DM me to discuss if the posted rates are outside your budget.
What if I'm not satisfied with the service? +
Reach out to me directly and I'll do my best to make it right. I stand by the quality of my work and communication is always open.
